Recently Completed Project:

A Modern Patio with Historic Elegance

This month's featured project perfectly blends contemporary design with the timeless character of a historic home. Here's the Before, Design, and After:

  • Before: A small, outdated patio with a closed-off courtyard feel around a modern pool. The space lacked cohesion with the historic architecture of the home.
  • Design: The challenge was to create a sleek, modern patio that seamlessly tied into the old-world charm of the property. We selected Unilock's modern pavers in complementary colors and textures, carefully integrating them into the design. The fire pit area features natural boulders in the landscaping, adding warmth and character, while a unique inlay of contrasting pavers provides a visually striking focal point.
  • After: The backyard is now a luxurious, multifunctional retreat where the family can create memories and entertain guests in style. The expansive patio, dramatic fireplace, and thoughtfully designed spaces make this project truly one-of-a-kind.

Award Recognition:This project was a finalist at the prestigious Unilock Awards, celebrated as one of the best "Before and After" transformations. We're honored to have our work recognized on such a grand stage!
