Recently Completed Project:

A Stunning Front Yard Transformation to Start the Year!

Check out this recent front yard renovation project we completed in St. Louis Hills, MO! We transformed a lackluster yard into an eye-catching, welcoming space with custom design elements and expert craftsmanship. Here's a closer look at the transformation:

  • Before: A space with a few overgrown bushes and little life, offering no curb appeal or functionality.
  • Design: We created a detailed 3D design featuring a paver walkway, big boulders for added character, and a lush landscape plan complete with vibrant plantings and strategically placed landscape lighting.
  • After: The backyard is now a luxurious, multifunctional retreat where the family can create memories and entertain guests in style. The expansive patio, dramatic fireplace, and thoughtfully designed spaces make this project truly one-of-a-kind.

Award Recognition:The results are breathtaking! The yard now features a beautiful paver walkway leading to the front entrance, stunning boulders that add depth and interest, a lush lawn, and landscape lighting that highlights the new features and extends usability into the evening.
