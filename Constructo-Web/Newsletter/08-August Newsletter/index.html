Recently Completed Project:

A Small Backyard Turned Outdoor Oasis

This month's featured project transformed a small, uncomfortable backyard into a functional and inviting oasis. Here's the Before, Design, and After:

  • Before: The yard featured an old, oversized deck that consumed valuable space, a cracked and uneven concrete patio, and no shade to escape the summer heat. The space was underutilized and lacked charm.
  • Design: We reimagined the backyard by creating a flowing paver patio with curvy landscaped beds to soften the space. A covered gazebo with a ceiling fan was added for shade and comfort, along with an outdoor kitchen for barbeques. A bubbling water feature became the centerpiece of the yard, providing a calming focal point. We also incorporated a custom media package with an outdoor TV and speakers to bring entertainment outdoors.
  • After: The transformation is a perfect blend of style and functionality. The new patio and gazebo create a comfortable space for family gatherings, while the lush landscaping and the water feature offer a peaceful retreat. The yard now feels larger, more inviting, and perfect for summer relaxation or entertaining guests.
