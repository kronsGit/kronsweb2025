Recently Completed Project:

From Unusable Hillside to Ultimate Party Space

This December, we're highlighting a challenging yet rewarding transformation. Here's how we gave our client a safe, functional, and beautiful backyard:

  • Before: A dangerously steep hillside made the backyard completely unusable. The client had no space for gatherings or even casual outdoor relaxation.
  • Design: Our solution involved building multiple retaining walls to create functional levels, each with its own purpose. We incorporated a large paver patio, a fire pit area with a custom veneer seating bench, and a privacy wall equipped with an outdoor TV.
  • After: The transformation is astounding! The client now enjoys a spacious, multi-level backyard perfect for hosting large parties, relaxing by the fire, or watching their favorite shows outdoors. Best of all, the space is safe and easy to navigate, offering peace of mind and endless enjoyment.
