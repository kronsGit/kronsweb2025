Recently Completed Project:

A Modern Front Yard Transformation for a Hillside Home

Check out this breathtaking front yard renovation we completed for a newly constructed home! The steep hillside presented unique challenges, but we delivered a solution that is both functional and visually stunning. Here's the Before, Design, and After:

  • Before: A steep, underutilized hillside that limited access and curb appeal.
  • Design: We incorporated a smooth, modern retaining wall to hold back the hillside and free up space for a sleek paver walkway. The design emphasized clean lines and contrasting colors to complement the home's contemporary architecture.
  • After: The transformation is remarkable! The new retaining wall not only stabilizes the hillside but also serves as a stunning focal point. The paver walkway pops against the landscape, and the under-cap lighting and path lights add elegance and safety. Low-maintenance landscaping completes the polished look.
