Recently Completed Project:

A Multi-Level City Oasis for Entertaining

In this month's featured project, we transformed a simple grass backyard into a stunning multi-level entertainment haven. Here's the Before, Design, and After:

  • Before: A small city backyard with nothing but grass and limited usability. The space lacked character, comfort, and functionality for outdoor gatherings.
  • Design: We created a multi-level design to maximize the space's potential. The upper level features a sleek paver patio perfect for outdoor dining. A set of steps leads to the lower level, where we added a stylish outdoor structure complete with a full media package, including an outdoor TV and speakers for movie nights under the stars. The landscaping was kept simple and low maintenance to enhance the overall design without overwhelming the space.
  • After: The transformation is incredible! This once-plain backyard is now a functional and stylish retreat for dining, entertaining, and relaxing. The addition of shade, modern amenities, and thoughtful design details makes it a standout space in the heart of the city.
