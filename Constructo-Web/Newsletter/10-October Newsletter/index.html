Recently Completed Project:

From Limited Space to a Multi-Zone Hangout

This month's featured project showcases how a small backyard can be turned into the ultimate gathering spot. Here's the Before, Design, and After:

  • Before: A small yard with an old pergola, a patch of grass, and a cramped deck that occupied valuable space. The layout lacked functionality and charm.
  • Design: We relocated the deck stairs to free up the yard's footprint and created a spacious paver patio area with a new pergola for shade and style. To balance the hardscaping, we incorporated decorative beds with Mexican Beach Pebble and low-maintenance plantings, adding a serene and tranquil vibe.
  • After: The transformation is spectacular! The space now features multiple zones perfect for entertaining friends and family, blending hardscape and softscape elements to create a peaceful backyard retreat.
